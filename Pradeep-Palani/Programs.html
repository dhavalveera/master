Our Programs

Infant Care


Our infant program is full day service which offers a customized routine for every infant allowing the baby opportunities for learning through Music, Gross Motor Time, Reading Time, and Play with Toys. We follow Parent Instruction on Food & Nap schedules for each baby.



Ratios :

 

1:3 Teacher - Child Ratio.


Staff :

Trained in early childhood care & education although Kidz R Us's Proprietary training program. Also certified in emergency medical care through reowned hospitals. A bright young team whom babies love to be with and learn from.



Pre-School


A vibrant and fun programme that helps children start off on a lifetime journey of learning. The day is filled with enriching activities that allow holistic development. We have an internationally acclaimed assessment program that clearly marks out how the child is developing so we can customize learning to maximum her potential.



Ratios :

 

1:6 Teacher - Child Ratio.


Staff :

Graduates & Post-Graduates with strong background training in ECE, along with a certification in emergency medical aid. Our teaching staff are given training round the year to ensure that they are one step ahead when it comes to children & their learning needs.



Day Care


Our day care program combines our preschool and after school programs for every child. along with the routine-based freed and nap time schedules integrated in the day. What follows is a vibrant, fun filled day for the child, with enough rest to charge them up. No wonder some of them want to come to Kidz R Us even on weekends!



Ratios :

 

1:6 Teacher - Child Ratio.


Staff :

Graduates and post graduates with a strong background training in ECE, along with a certification in emergency medical aid. Our teaching staff are given training round the year to ensure that they are one step ahead when it comes to children and their learning needs.

Cares :

Children at our centers are given healthy, wholesome Snacks designed by reowned nutritionists. They have a stimulating routine which includes nap time, grooming, outdoor, and fun activities during the day.



After Schools Program


Kidz R Us after school programs offer a variety of activities that complement learning in Schools. With fun activities such as Book Club, Gardening, Science Experiements & Storytelling sessions. Children find their school lessions come alive through our after school curriculum.



Ratios :

 

1:8 Teacher - Child Ratio.


Staff :

Graduates & Post-Graduates with a strong background training in ECE, along with a certification in emergency medical aid. Among our teaching staff, we also have Dancers, Musicians and other talented artists who ensure that afters choolers are rounded in the arts.

Our teaching staff are given training round the year to ensure that they are a step ahead in caring for children.
