Why "Kidz R Us ?"

Kidz R Us was born out of the belief that a women can have it all - a fulfilling career and loving, Happy Children. At Kidz R Us, we create and manage high quality preschool daycares so women can discard guilt and embrace joy. Read on to know what sets us apart.



Slogan :- Your Kids are Our Kids

Safety - an Obession
Our Centre design - spacious & child-friendly. Our toys - branded & confirmed to international safety standards.




Hygiene - A Habit
With one dedicated cleaning staff in each classroom just to clean, our spaces are spotless & germ-free.




Care & Learning - A Passion
1:3 teaching ratios for infants and 1:6 for children - when you have such high levels of attention..
