Curriculum



It is not about how smart you are, it is about How you are Smart
- Howard Gardner

Kidz R Us well researched & International World of Discovery curriculum was developed after 2 years of research by a dedicated team of Expert Child Psychologist, pediatricians, early childhood educators & researchers.



The World of Discovery curriculum combines the best practice of internationally reowned curriculum methods. Our children practice immersive learning through a theme-based approach. We expose children to various activities across a single theme, so they learn through their own strenghts and interests. Our children also develop a strong exposure across multiple areas, and get geared to become all-rounders.




The World of Discovery curriculum Accelerating learning experience.



✓ We build a Caring & Loving Environment.


✓ We provide a child the freedom to explore and experiment


✓ We ensure attention to all aspects of a child's early development - Physical, Cognitive, Social, Emotional, Sensorial & Language.


✓ We stimulate and encourage the child to have meaningful interaction with peers and adults.
