Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal in English it is Called "Palace of Winds" or "Palace of the Breeze".
Palace is Located Jaipur, India.
The Strcture was Build in 1799 by Maharaja "Sawai Pratap Singh"


India Gate

The India Gate originally called the All India War Memorial
India Gate is memorial of 82,000 soliders of the Indian Army who died in the Period of 1914 - 1921 in the First World War.


Golden Temple

Golden Temple is holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism
This Gurdwara is Located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India.
Golden Temple was founded in 1577 by the fourth Sikh guru Guru Ram Das.
